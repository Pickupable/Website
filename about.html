About Us

Glasgow Financial Advice is an independent financial advisory firm serving the families and individuals of Glasgow. We are committed to providing clear, honest, and personalised investment advice — free from the conflicts of interest that can affect tied or restricted advisers.

As independent advisers, we are able to consider the whole of the market when making recommendations, ensuring that every client receives a solution suited precisely to their circumstances.

Our Philosophy

Independence

We are not tied to any provider. Our recommendations are based solely on what is right for you.

Transparency

All fees and charges are explained clearly before any advice is given. No hidden costs, no surprises.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest professional and ethical standards in everything we do.

Regulatory Status

Glasgow Financial Advice is authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA). You can verify our registration at any time via the FCA's Financial Services Register.

We operate in full compliance with the FCA's Conduct of Business Sourcebook (COBS), including our obligations under the Consumer Duty to deliver good outcomes for retail clients.
