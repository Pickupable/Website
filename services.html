Our Services

Comprehensive financial planning tailored to the needs of Glasgow families and NHS workers.

Critical Illness Cover

A critical illness diagnosis can be life-changing — financially as well as personally. Critical illness cover pays a tax-free lump sum on diagnosis of a specified serious illness, providing the financial breathing room to focus on recovery without worrying about your mortgage, your family, or your income.

This is a product we believe in deeply. Having experienced a critical illness claim first-hand, we understand the difference it makes when cover is in place — and the vulnerability that comes when it is not.

Income Protection

Your ability to earn is your most valuable financial asset. Income protection insurance pays a regular, tax-free monthly income if you are unable to work due to illness or injury — bridging the gap when employer sick pay runs out.

This is particularly important for NHS workers, whose sick pay is capped at six months full pay followed by six months half pay — with nothing thereafter. We can structure a policy to complement your NHS employment terms precisely.

Life Insurance

Protecting the people who depend on you financially is one of the most important steps any family can take. We advise on level and decreasing term life insurance, ensuring your cover is appropriate for your mortgage, your dependants, and your circumstances — and that it works alongside any death-in-service benefit provided by your employer.

Investment Planning

We work with you to understand your financial objectives, your attitude to risk, and your investment time horizon. From this, we construct a bespoke investment strategy using St. James's Place's range of investment solutions, managed by some of the world's leading fund managers.

We review your investments regularly and adjust your strategy as your circumstances change.

Pension Planning

Whether you are beginning to plan for retirement or approaching it, we provide clear and practical pension advice. We can review your existing arrangements, advise on contributions, and plan your income in retirement — including navigating the complexities of the NHS Pension Scheme, annual allowance charges, and the McCloud remedy for NHS members.

ISA & Tax-Efficient Savings

Making the most of your annual ISA allowance is one of the simplest ways to grow your wealth tax-efficiently. We advise on Stocks & Shares ISAs and Junior ISAs to help your family build long-term financial security alongside your pension.

Estate Planning

Ensuring your wealth passes to the people you care about — efficiently and according to your wishes — requires careful planning. We advise on inheritance tax planning, trusts, and powers of attorney to protect your estate and your family's future.
